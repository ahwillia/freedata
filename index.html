List of Open Access, High-Dimensional Neural Time Series

More and more neuroscience labs are deciding to share datasets online after initial publication. This list aims to collect open access datasets with a substantial number of simultaneously recorded neurons (10 or more). How to characterize these high-dimensional time series is still a very open question from a statistical modeling perspective.

Note: this page is a work in progress.
